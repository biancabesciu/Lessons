Favourite Band

Muse are an English rock band from Teignmouth, Devon, formed in 1994. The band consists of Matt Bellamy (lead vocals, guitar, piano, keyboards), Chris Wolstenholme (bass guitar, backing vocals, keyboards) and Dominic Howard (drums, percussion).

Favourite albums

  • Absolution (2003)
  • Black Holes and Revelations (2006)

Favorite songs:

  1. "Suppermasive Black Hole"
  2. "Starlight"
  3. "Knights of Cydonia"
  4. "Hysteria"
  5. "Mercy"
  6. "Madness"
  7. "Dead inside"

In 2015, I finally got to see them in concert. They performed at the Rock Werchter festival.
