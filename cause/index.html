Oceans

Our big blue backyard

Our planet is blessed with something special...

We've got barrier reefs
Striking coastlines
Our oceans relax and sustain us.
Uplift and inspire us.
We're captivated by
our lovely marine friends.
...the big
...and the small

But some think our oceans exist to be exploited.
Leaving too much damage

...that we can't ignore

Our oceans are in trouble.
Three quarters of fish stocks are over-exploited or fished to their limit. Marine animals are being caught, killed or driven from their homes.

Thankfully, there are people who want to change that.
Together we can change the tide!
